FAQ

Common Questions

Do you come to me, or do I bring my car?
We come to you. Atlas Studio is 100% mobile we work at your home, office, or wherever your vehicle is parked in the Las Vegas area.
How long does a Reset take?
Interior Reset: 3–5 hours. Paint Correction Reset: 5–8 hours. Full Reset is typically a full day. We'll give you a specific estimate based on your vehicle's condition.
What's the difference between a wash and a Reset?
A wash removes surface dirt. A Reset removes paint defects swirls, scratches, oxidation and restores your vehicle to near-showroom condition. Completely different result.
Why is Maintenance post-correction only?
Maintenance protects a corrected surface. Starting maintenance on uncorrected paint means maintaining defects not results. We reset first, then maintain properly.
What vehicles do you work on?
Everything from daily drivers to exotics. Sedans, SUVs, trucks, and luxury vehicles all welcome. Exotics are quoted individually.
What if my vehicle is excessively dirty?
Starting prices assume average condition. If your vehicle has heavy soiling, pet hair, strong odors, biohazard, or serious neglect we'll let you know upfront and adjust the quote accordingly. No surprises.
How do I pay?
Payment after the service is complete and you've done your walk-through. We accept Venmo, Zelle, cash, and card. The final number is confirmed before we start.
