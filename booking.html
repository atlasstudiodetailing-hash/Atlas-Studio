FAQ

Common Questions

Do you come to me?
Yes. Atlas Studio is 100% mobile. We work at your home, office, or wherever your vehicle is parked in the Las Vegas area.
How long does a Reset take?
Interior Reset: 3–5 hours. Paint Correction: 5–8 hours. Full Reset is typically a full day. We give you a specific estimate based on your vehicle.
What if my vehicle is excessively dirty?
Starting prices assume average condition. Heavy soiling, pet hair, strong odors, or serious neglect will be factored into your quote upfront. No surprises.
What is the difference between a wash and a Reset?
A wash removes surface dirt. A Reset removes paint defects — swirls, scratches, oxidation — and restores your vehicle to near-showroom condition. Completely different result.
Why is Maintenance post-correction only?
Maintenance protects a corrected surface. Starting maintenance on uncorrected paint means maintaining defects, not results. We reset first, then maintain properly.
How do I pay?
Payment after the service is complete and you have done your walk-through. We accept Venmo, Zelle, cash, and card. The final number is confirmed before we start.
