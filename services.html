Vegas Sun Reality

What Vegas Heat Does to Your Paint

UV intensity in Las Vegas is significantly higher than most US cities. Here is what actually happens to unprotected paint over time.

Week 1–4
Clear coat oxidation begins. UV rays break down the protective layer invisibly.
Invisible — but happening
Month 3
First swirl marks appear after wash contact. Gloss starts to flatten in direct sun.
Visible in bright light
Year 1
Paint oxidation visible to the naked eye. Deep scratches. Color fading begins.
Obvious to buyers
Year 2–3
$3,000–$8,000 in resale value lost. Professional correction or full repaint required.
Costly to reverse

A paint correction now costs a fraction of what oxidation damage costs later.
